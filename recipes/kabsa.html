Kabsa

Description

This Arabic dish is very beloved and is cooked in many arab countries.

There are slight variations in the way that its cooked, but generaly its always cooked with meat or chicken

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat or Chicken
  • spices -cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, black lime
  • love

Steps

  1. Fry onions and spices in oil
  2. Add meat/chicken and cook until browned
  3. Mix in tomatoes, then add water/broth
  4. Add rice, cover, and simmer until tender
